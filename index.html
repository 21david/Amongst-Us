Instructions

The ship is falling apart and it's up to you to save it! Run around the ship and finish all the tasks to save the ship before time runs out! Tasks are indicated by green rectangles on the floor. Stand on one and click the "Start task" button or press "space" to open the task screen and complete the task. Press "Esc" or "Q" to exit out of a task screen. Move around with the arrow keys or W, A, S, D keys. Finish quickly!
